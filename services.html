Proving Ground Testing

We execute test campaigns across global proving grounds to simulate real-world duty cycles. Services include:

  • Durability loops and abuse tests
  • Ride and handling assessments
  • Thermal validation and hill climbs
  • Instrumentation and on-site monitoring

Road Load Data Acquisition (RLDA)

Accurate and complete RLDA programs drive all downstream simulations. Our services cover:

  • Sensor installation and calibration (strain, acceleration, pressure)
  • CAN/LIN data capture and synchronization
  • High-frequency data logging (up to 10 kHz)
  • Post-processing: rainflow counting, signal compression, time history editing

Shaker Table Testing

Lab-based replication of field conditions using single and multi-axis input. Our shaker test services include:

  • Sine Sweep and Random Vibration test profiles
  • Shock response spectrum (SRS) testing
  • Component durability fatigue using RLDA-based signals

Simulation & CAE

Our simulation portfolio covers full vehicle and subsystem level FEA/CFD with correlation to test data. Capabilities:

  • Static and dynamic FEA for strength and stiffness
  • Fatigue life estimation using nCode or fe-safe
  • Thermal and vibrational NVH simulations
  • Vehicle dynamics & multi-body modeling
