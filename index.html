Welcome to RKH Engineering

Founded by Somnath Ghorad, RKH Engineering offers specialized services in the automotive and mobility sectors. We focus on advanced vehicle testing, precise road load data acquisition, and high-fidelity simulations for both component and system-level evaluations. With years of hands-on experience and cutting-edge tools, we help clients bring durability and performance-driven designs to market efficiently.

Simulation
